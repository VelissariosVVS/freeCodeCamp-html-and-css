Clocktower Garden Opens Beneath the Morning Fog

A quiet corner of the waterfront is transformed into a glowing public retreat.

By Alex Belis

Residents gathered at sunrise as the old clocktower courtyard reopened with winding paths, brass benches, and lanterns that flicker softly after dusk.

City planners say the garden will host small concerts, weekend sketching circles, and seasonal flower displays throughout the year.
